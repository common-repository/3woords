Get a 3woords word cloud on your site. Ask your question and have visitors answer it with 3 words. The answers are presented as a word cloud.

== Description ==
Integrate 3woords on your webpage through our API.
What is 3woords?
AskÂ any question to your website visitors.
Let your visitors answer the question in three words. 
Summarize the answers in a word cloud based on artificial intelligence.
3woords combines state-of-the-art technology from artificial intelligence and statistics with research from psychology and psychometrics to create social surveys based on words, rather than rating scales.
== Installation ==
1. Install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the Settings-> 3woords screen to configure the plugin. You get the access-key and access-token from http://api.3woords.com/.
4. Write your questions and give it a code that are different from any other 3woords question on the same page.
